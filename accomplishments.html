Accomplishments

Milestones beyond the lab — endurance challenges, summits, awards and community work that have shaped my approach to discipline, teamwork and resilience.

Half-Marathon, London, UK

13.1 miles of persistence, preparation, and mental resilience. Training alongside research taught me the value of discipline, time management, and pushing through discomfort. Grateful for the challenge and the reminder that consistent effort pays off — in science, sport, and beyond.

May 2024

Summiting Pen y Fan, Wales, UK

Climbing Pen y Fan was all about teamwork and shared effort. Encouraging each other along the way showed how much collaboration, communication, and group resilience can make a difference. Whether it's on a mountain or in work, having the right people around you makes the journey easier and more rewarding.

March 2023

Summiting Kilimanjaro, Tanzania

Awarded a mountain climbing certificate for summiting Mount Kilimanjaro, the highest peak in Africa. Reaching the top required determination, resilience, and strong teamwork. The expedition raised £3,000 for charity.

September 2022

Higher Education Award, Guildford, UK

Received a Higher Education Award from the Society for Radiological Protection (SRP), which supports the advancement of radiation protection. Grateful for their sponsorship of my work and their continued commitment to promoting excellence in the field.

August 2020

Volunteering for Costa Coffee Foundation, London, UK

Proud to have spent the week volunteering with the Costa Coffee Foundation, distributing books to children in need. Access to books can profoundly shape a child's confidence, creativity, and future. Supporting literacy and education aligns closely with my values, and it was a privilege to contribute to this initiative.

April 2019

Physics Olympiad Award, London, UK

Looking back to the early stages of my career, I'm proud to have earned a silver medal in the Physics Olympiad. This achievement not only marked a significant moment in my academic journey but also fueled my passion for physics and solidified my commitment to pursuing a career in science.

March 2018

Excellence Award, London, UK

Honored to have received the Excellence Award from Strode's College. This recognition was awarded for my dedication to maintaining an exceptional work ethic and for the development of strong organizational skills. It was an important step in my journey, reinforcing the value of discipline, responsibility, and continuous improvement.
